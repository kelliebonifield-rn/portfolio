AI-Powered Clinical Coding Implementation

The Problem

Clinical coders were spending excessive time on manual code assignment, creating bottlenecks in the revenue cycle. The organization needed to improve coder productivity while maintaining accuracy and compliance.

My Approach

1. User Research: Conducted interviews with 15+ clinical coders to understand pain points, workflow patterns, and resistance factors.

2. Technology Assessment: Evaluated AI coding solutions and selected technology that could integrate with existing systems while providing explainable recommendations.

3. Pilot Program: Designed a phased rollout starting with a pilot team, gathering continuous feedback to refine the product.

4. Change Management: Created training materials and support systems to ensure smooth adoption.

Results

  • ✓ 45% improvement in coder efficiency
  • ✓ $120K quarterly savings through standardization
  • ✓ 95% user satisfaction after 90 days
  • ✓ Maintained 99%+ accuracy rate

Key Learnings

Success required balancing AI capabilities with user trust. By involving coders early, making AI decisions transparent, and positioning the technology as an assistant rather than replacement, we achieved strong adoption and measurable results.

Episode IQ Product Launch

The Problem

Back office staff were managing episodes manually across multiple systems, leading to inefficiencies and user frustration. The company needed its first internally-developed SaaS solution to address this gap.

My Approach

1. Product Discovery: Led workshops with back office teams to identify highest-impact pain points and prioritize features.

2. User-Centered Design: Created prototypes and conducted usability testing with 20+ users before development.

3. Agile Development: Worked closely with engineering to deliver MVP in 4-month sprint cycles with continuous user feedback.

4. Go-to-Market: Developed training materials, onboarding flows, and support documentation.

Results

  • ✓ 2 hours per week saved per user
  • ✓ First internal SaaS product launched successfully
  • ✓ 85% adoption rate within 6 months
  • ✓ Foundation for product line expansion

Key Learnings

Building internal products requires the same rigor as external products. Early user involvement, rapid iteration, and focusing on the highest-value use cases ensured we delivered a solution that users actually wanted to use.

M&A Integration: RCM Services

The Challenge

Following an acquisition, we needed to integrate RCM services into WellSky's technology platform while achieving cost synergies and maintaining service quality for existing customers.

My Approach

1. Systems Assessment: Mapped all existing systems, workflows, and dependencies across both organizations.

2. Roadmap Development: Created phased integration plan balancing quick wins with long-term platform consolidation.

3. Cross-Functional Leadership: Led teams across product, engineering, operations, and customer success through the integration.

4. Risk Mitigation: Identified and planned for potential customer impact, maintaining transparent communication throughout.

Results

  • ✓ $40K quarterly synergies achieved
  • ✓ Ahead of schedule delivery
  • ✓ Zero customer churn during integration
  • ✓ Unified platform for future growth

Key Learnings

Integration success depends on clear communication, realistic timelines, and keeping the customer experience front and center. By treating the integration as a product launch with defined milestones and success metrics, we delivered results ahead of expectations.
